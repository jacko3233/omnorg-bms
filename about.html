What is Omnor BMS?

Omnor Group delivers specialised engineering and logistics solutions across the UK and offshore sectors. Our BMS portal is designed to streamline project management, job tracking, equipment logistics, and subcontractor coordination.

Key Service Areas:

  • Fabrication & Welding
  • Plant & Equipment Hire
  • Lifting Operations
  • Load Testing
  • Transport & Logistics
  • Emergency Power & Support
